  <section class="profile-section" aria-labelledby="selected-publications">
    <div class="section-heading">
      <h2 id="selected-publications">Selected Publications</h2>
    </div>
    <div class="card-grid">
      <article class="profile-card">
        <p class="card-meta">IEEE Conference on Games (CoG), 2026</p>
        <h3>Foundation Models as World Models: A Foundational Study in Text-Based GridWorlds</h3>
        <p class="card-authors"><strong>Remo Sasso</strong>, Michelangelo Conserva, Dominik Jeurissen, Paulo Rauber</p>
        <p>A study of whether foundation models can act as world models in controlled text-based reinforcement learning environments, examining transition modelling, planning-relevant reasoning, and limitations in closed-loop decision-making.</p>
      </article>

      <article class="profile-card">
        <p class="card-meta">International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML), 2023</p>
        <h3>Posterior Sampling for Deep Reinforcement Learning</h3>
        <p class="card-authors"><strong>Remo Sasso</strong>, Michelangelo Conserva, Paulo Rauber</p>
        <p>Introduces PSDRL, a scalable approximation of posterior sampling for deep RL that combines latent dynamics models, Bayesian uncertainty estimation, and continual value-function-based planning for sample-efficient exploration.</p>
        <p class="card-links"><a href="https://arxiv.org/pdf/2305.00477.pdf" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Paper</a><a href="https://github.com/remosasso/PSDRL" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Code</a></p>
      </article>

      <article class="profile-card">
        <p class="card-meta">Transactions on Machine Learning Research (TMLR), 2023</p>
        <h3>Multi-Source Transfer Learning for Deep Model-Based Reinforcement Learning</h3>
        <p class="card-authors"><strong>Remo Sasso</strong>, Matthia Sabatelli, Marco Wiering</p>
        <p>Studies transfer learning across source environments for deep model-based reinforcement learning, with a focus on sample-efficient dynamics learning. Also presented at the NeurIPS 2022 Deep Reinforcement Learning Workshop.</p>
        <p class="card-links"><a href="https://arxiv.org/pdf/2205.14410.pdf" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Paper</a><a href="https://github.com/remosasso/multi-source-TL-for-deep-MBRL" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Code</a></p>
      </article>
    </div>
